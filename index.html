About Me

I am a web developer, with an eye for clean and efficient design. I specialize in crafting high quality, intuitive user experiences.

Technologies I code with include: HTML5, CSS3 (SASS/SCSS), Javascript (ES6, jQuery, React), Git and Sketch.

With coding, I take a similar approach to my craft as a basketball player. A basketball player finds purpose in endlessly practicing their shooting form, or their free throws, etc. In my coding work I find motivation in the logic, creativity and learning opportunities it provides.

I recently delved deeper into CSS Grid, and tested out each of its properties. I particularly love the grid-template-areas property, which adds a new level of syntax, in adding names to the areas of your grids. I have been enjoying React lately as well. As part of the initial learning process, I read and reread lots of documentation until I could truly discern the differences between props and state.
